#### During the Event

Additional guidelines from Siemens:

##### Personal Belongings

It is strongly advised that you leave all non-essential belongings at your hotel or in your car. There are no lockers or storage for laptop bags and luggage at the facility. This means that you'll have to keep these items with you at all times, including the facility tours. Please keep this in mind when planning your visit!

##### Building Access and Security

Upon arrival, all visitors will enter the main entrance and check in at the reception desk. All guests are required to wear an assigned Visitor badge or Company ID when in the facility. It should be visible at all times. This includes Siemens and non-Siemens guests. Please note due to many on-going research projects in the facility, access to certain areas of the building will be restricted.

##### Photography and Recording

Use of cameras or recording devices in and around the site is not permitted unless there is an authorized exception.

##### Smoking Policy

The Princeton location is a smoke-free building. For anyone that wishes to smoke or vape, there is a designated location at the end of the sidewalk by the employee entrance.
